Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexusChain

NexusChain is a next-generation Layer 1 blockchain platform designed for scalability, security, and true decentralization. We provide infrastructure for building decentralized applications, DeFi protocols, and enterprise solutions.

Getting started is easy! Simply connect your Web3 wallet, acquire NXC tokens from supported exchanges, and start exploring our ecosystem. For developers, check out our documentation and SDK to begin building.

NexusChain combines innovative consensus mechanisms with advanced sharding technology to achieve unprecedented throughput while maintaining decentralization. Our developer-first approach and comprehensive tooling make building on NexusChain seamless.

Security is our top priority. Our protocol has undergone multiple audits by leading security firms, and we maintain a bug bounty program. Our proof-of-stake consensus ensures economic security with minimal energy consumption.

NXC token holders can participate in on-chain governance by voting on proposals, suggesting improvements, and delegating their voting power. Our DAO structure ensures community-driven development and decision-making.
